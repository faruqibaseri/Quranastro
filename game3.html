Welcome to the Word Search!🚀

Let's have some fun finding hidden words! Focus your eyes and spot all the amazing words about space.

How to Play 🧭

  • Drag from the first letter to the last letter to select a word.
  • Words can appear horizontally, vertically, or diagonally (forwards & backwards).
  • Use Hint to reveal the first letter (costs 5s).
  • All found words will be marked FOUND.
