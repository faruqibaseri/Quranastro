How to Play 🧭

  • Each level contains a sentence with several blanks. Drag answers into blanks.
  • Choices include extra distractors — think carefully.
  • Fill all blanks, then press Check Answers. Correct fillings give points and advance to next level.
  • Wrong drop gives a small time penalty. Beat levels to get a high score.
