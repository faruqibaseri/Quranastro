Orbit Alignment

Welcome to Orbit Alignment! 🚀

Drag celestial objects (Moon, Star, Planet) onto the correct orbit around the Sun. Beat the timer and rack up points!

How to Play 🧭

  • Sun stays in the center. There are three orbits: Inner (Moon), Middle (Star), Outer (Planet).
  • Drag items from the tray into the correct orbit slots.
  • Correct drop = score + time bonus. Wrong drop = -5s penalty.
  • Finish when all slots are correctly filled.
