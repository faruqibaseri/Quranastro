Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To inspire curiosity and appreciation of the universe from the Sun, Moon, and Stars by integrating astronomy and Qur’anic knowledge into an engaging digital learning platform.

Our Mission

To build an engaging learning space that blends Qur’anic ayat, astronomy, and the wonders of the Sun, Moon, and Stars through interactive media for students and lifelong learners.

Frequently Asked Questions

QuranAstro is an educational platform that explores astronomy from both scientific and Islamic perspectives. It covers the wonders of the moon, the sun, and the stars, showing their roles in the universe and their significance in the Quran.

QuranAstro is for students, teachers, researchers, and anyone curious about the wonders of the universe. It is especially useful for those who want to learn about the moon, sun, and stars from both science and Islamic viewpoints.

The main sources used in this website are Quranic verses, tafsir 'Ilmi (Islamic exegesis) and scientific references in astronomy.

Of course! The resources here are designed to support learning, research, and discovery. You can use QuranAstro for school projects, assignments, and other related activities!
